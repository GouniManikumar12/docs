# Advertiser Overview

AdMesh connects your brand to real, high-intent audiences inside AI chats, search assistants, and agent platforms.  
Your products appear exactly when users ask for something you offer ,  making every impression contextual and relevant.

You control:

- **Offers** – what you promote  
- **Budgets & Payouts** – how much you spend  
- **Goals** – whether you focus on clicks or conversions  

AdMesh ensures transparency, brand safety, and measurable ROI ,  without the complexity of traditional ads.
