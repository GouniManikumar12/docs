## Sign Up to Get Started

Ready to promote your products inside AI conversations?

**[Sign up at useadmesh.com](https://useadmesh.com)** to create your advertiser account and start reaching high-intent users in AI chats, search assistants, and agent platforms.

---

Get started in three simple steps.

## 1. Create Your Offer

Add your product name, short description, and landing page.  
Choose your goal ,  pay **per click (CPC)** or **per conversion (CPA)**.

## 2. Set Your Budget

Decide how much you want to spend overall and per day.  
You can pause, edit, or adjust rates anytime.

## 3. Go Live

Once your offer is approved, it instantly becomes eligible to appear in AI-powered search results and conversations.  
AdMesh automatically handles placements, targeting, and performance tracking.
