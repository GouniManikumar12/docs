Your AdMesh dashboard gives you complete visibility into campaign performance.

## Key Metrics

- Exposures, clicks, and conversions  
- Cost per click / cost per action  
- Total spend and ROI  
- Top queries, platforms, and audiences  
- Daily and weekly trends  

You can filter by date range, export data as CSV, or receive automated email summaries.
